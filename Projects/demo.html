Description

Parkinson Disease Assesment Portal

Description

A portal for assessing Parkinson's disease using pre trained LLMs and transformers. The portal also featuers a digital twin feature to customise the experience for each individual patient and provide more accurate assesments. RAG is also used with dataset from PPMI to provide accurate reports and insights.
